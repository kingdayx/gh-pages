Project Name: OTR Bake Sale

Description: Recreating the ethereum dapp from studio.ethereum.org into a dapp made with React. It is a bake sale for OTR's new coin. It allows the user to name there pizza and choose their toppings, purchase the pizza, send the pizza as a gift with a given ethereum address, and eat the pizza. The pizzas are collectibles with ERC721. It is made with React, Solidity, Truffle, and Web3.
